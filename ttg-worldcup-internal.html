Rules & Mechanics
How it all works
Your 4 teams
You score points from ALL 4 of your assigned teams combined. If two of your teams play each other, you score points for whichever one wins, draws, scores, or earns bonuses — both sides count.
Tracking
Points are updated after every match day. The scorekeeper posts updates to the group chat and the leaderboard here reflects the latest totals.
Tier definitions
Tier 1 = Spain, France, England, Portugal, Brazil, Argentina, Germany, Netherlands, Belgium, Norway, Colombia, Mexico. Tier 2 = Morocco through Australia. These are locked from the draw and used for all upset bonus calculations.
Tiebreakers
If two players are tied on points: (1) most combined wins across all 4 teams, then (2) most combined goals scored, then (3) coin flip at the prize ceremony.
Golden Boot-Out eligibility
Must have actively participated — at least one reaction or comment in the group chat per stage. Stops someone from going silent and accidentally winning the consolation prize.
Drama points
Comeback wins (+5), added-time goals (+4), and shootout wins (+3) count toward total points AND the Most Dramatic award separately. Scorekeeper keeps a side tally.
Prize ceremony
Three moments: after group stage, after quarterfinals, and a Grand Final celebration on or around July 19.
